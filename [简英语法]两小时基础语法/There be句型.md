# There be句型

## 一.什么是there be句型?

**表达"某个地方有......"**

---

## there be句子翻译

桌子上有一台红色的电脑

> There is a red computer on the table

车上有很多人

> There are many people in the car

这里有一些书

> There are some books here

那里有一个女孩

There is a girl there

床上有一只狗

> There is a dog on the bed

---

数量为1 there is

数量大于1 there are
